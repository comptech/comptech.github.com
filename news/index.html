Technical Development News

  • Thu 15 January 2015

    • First meeting with COMPTECH technical advisory board (TAB) in 2015. Dr. Quentin Williams, Dr. Yanbin Wang, Dr. Guoyin Shen, Dr. Lars Ehm, Dr. Robert Downs and COMPRES president Dr. Jay Bass joined the meeting with Dr. Przemek Dera and Dr. Jin Zhang. The meeting brought in lots of insightful suggestions and comments, ensured the COMPTECH project moving toward the correct direction.

  • Fri 9 January 2015

    • Dr. Jin Zhang finished the design of the first version universal membrane cap, which is a single-membrane DAC cap ideal for precise remote pressure control during compressional process. It fits most DACs people used in high-pressure community without losing angular opening, including Princeton type and Japanese piston cylinder DAC, modified 3-pin and 4-pin Merrill-Bassett-type DACs, BX90 DAC, etc.

  • Sun 14 December 2014

    • Initial collaboration with Dr. Ruqing Xu on TDS measurement at high-pressure conditions. Tests were made under ambient conditions on thin (~15 microns) samples comparable to DAC samples.

  • Mon 09 December 2013

    • The AGU fall meeting in San Francisco was held from December 9 to 14. Dr. Bin Chen presented his work on "Sound velocities of Fe7C3" at 9 am on Monday.

  • Tue 12 November 2013

    • Drs. Przemyslaw Dera and Bin Chen attended the EarthCube workshop on November 12-14, 2013 in Alexadria, VA. The 2.5-day workshop addressed the current state and future directions of cyberinfrastructure development in support of rock deformation and high pressure mineral physics research via the COMPRES and the DEFORM communitiies. The central goals of the workshop were to facilitate scientific discovery via cyberinfrastructure development and to identify EarthCube activities that promote research in high-pressure mineral physics and rock deformation.
