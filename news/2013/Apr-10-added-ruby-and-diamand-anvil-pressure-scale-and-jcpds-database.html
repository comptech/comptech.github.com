Added Ruby and diamand anvil pressure scale and JCPDS database

Published: Apr 10, 2013
Last updated: 2013-04-10 17:00:00 +0000

Ruby pressure scale and Diamond Anvil pressure calculator have been added to the tools section of the website. These two tools are written and maintained by Bin Chen (binchen2@illinois.edu binchen@hawaii.edu) or Jin Zhang (jinz@hawaii.edu).

Also, a JCPDS database is created. If you have any files to contribute, please contact Bin Chen (binchen@illinois.edu binchen@hawaii.edu) or Jin Zhang (jinz@hawaii.edu). Thank you!
